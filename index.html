Our innovative off-shore wind turbine solution can be broken down into 4 main parts:

  • Adaptive blades using biomimicry
  • 4D printing with polylactic acid
  • 3D printing wind turbine towers
  • Floating and off-shore wind turbines

Sustainable, Efficient & Affordable Energy!

Innovating a new generation of adaptable wind turbines.

Our floaitng off-shore wind turbines entail the use of 4D printing (an emerging technology) to build adaptive wind turbine blades by mimicking the structure of the plant=leaf veins. This idea ultimately provides more efficient and sustainable energy generation.

Our solution will allow us to create wind turbines that are 7.6x more efficient than the status quo.

Adaptive Wind Turbine Blades using Biomimicry

By mimicking the structure of plant leaf veins to design wind turbine blades, the blades will be able to adapt to different environmental stresses by changing shape to face less resistance; optimizing efficiency.

explore more

4D Printing Blades with Polylactic Acid

By 4D printing (an emerging field of study) using a smart material called polylactic acid (PLA), the turbines withstand high temperatures and wind speeds while being more cost-effective and sustainable.

explore more

3D Printing Wind Turbine Towers

We’re using a Gantry concrete 3D printer, to create the base and tower of the wind turbine at the installation location. This is a faster, cost-effective solution due to the significant reduction of manual labor and error.

explore more

Floating and off-shore wind turbines

By building floating off-shore wind turbines, we will be able to harness higher speeds of more consistent wind and more space; as well as the lack of physical interference that land or human-made objects can present.

explore more
